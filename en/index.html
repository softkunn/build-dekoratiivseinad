Frequently asked questions

Short answers about microcement, bathroom renovation, and our work.

What is microcement and when should I use it?

Microcement is a thin mineral finish for walls and floors, especially bathrooms and wet rooms. Applied correctly, it creates a seamless, waterproof surface without grout lines.

What does Dekoratiivseinad OÜ do?

We build and finish walls and bathrooms in Estonia: bathroom renovation, microcement finishes, decorative wall coatings (including clay plaster, lime plaster, and tadelakt), and related construction work.

How long does a bathroom renovation take?

Timing depends on room size and scope. We agree a clear schedule before starting and plan the work to keep downtime as short as possible.

Can microcement be used in an old bathroom?

Often yes, after the right surface prep. We assess the site and recommend whether the existing base needs work first.

How do I get a quote?

Briefly describe the job (e.g. microcement bathroom, area) in the contact form, or call / email us. We reply soon and can arrange a site visit if needed.
